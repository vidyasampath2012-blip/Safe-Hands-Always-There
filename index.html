SafeHands — Always There

"Because they cared for us — now it's our turn."

SafeHands is a mobile app designed to support elderly people living alone — ensuring their safety, health and emotional wellbeing — always.

💡 The Problem

  • 15 Crore people in India are above 60 years old
  • 3 Crore elderly people live completely alone
  • They struggle daily with medicines, meals and emergencies
  • No app offers regional language training
